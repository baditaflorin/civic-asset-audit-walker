# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project harassment-free for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Assume civic volunteers may be under time, safety, or political pressure.
- Keep discussion focused on improving the tool and protecting contributors.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Attempts to deanonymize volunteers or misuse audit data.

## Enforcement

Report concerns to baditaflorin@gmail.com. Maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributors that violate this code.
